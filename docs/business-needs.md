# Business Needs Document

## Executive Summary

The EISHRO platform addresses critical business needs in Libya's e-commerce landscape by providing a comprehensive, secure, and user-friendly marketplace that connects merchants with customers while ensuring regulatory compliance and technological excellence.

## Business Objectives

### Primary Objectives
1. **Market Leadership**: Become Libya's leading e-commerce platform within 24 months
2. **Merchant Growth**: Onboard 5,000+ active merchants by year-end 2026
3. **Customer Base**: Achieve 500,000+ registered customers within 18 months
4. **Revenue Target**: Generate LYD 15M+ annual revenue by 2027
5. **Market Share**: Capture 40% of Libya's e-commerce market share

### Secondary Objectives
1. **Regional Expansion**: Extend services to neighboring North African countries
2. **Technology Innovation**: Maintain technological leadership in the region
3. **Financial Inclusion**: Enable small businesses to participate in digital economy
4. **Employment Creation**: Generate 1,000+ jobs through platform ecosystem

## Market Analysis

### Current Market Landscape
**Libya E-commerce Market (2025):**
- **Market Size**: LYD 2.5B annual e-commerce transactions
- **Growth Rate**: 35% YoY growth
- **Mobile Commerce**: 78% of transactions via mobile devices
- **Payment Methods**: Cash on delivery (60%), digital payments (40%)
- **Major Challenges**: Trust, logistics, payment security, digital literacy

### Target Market Segments

#### Primary Segments
**Small & Medium Merchants:**
- 15,000+ retail businesses in Libya
- Annual revenue: LYD 50K - 2M
- Technology adoption: Low to medium
- Pain points: Limited reach, manual operations, cash flow issues

**Individual Entrepreneurs:**
- 50,000+ aspiring entrepreneurs
- Age group: 25-45 years
- Skills: Basic digital literacy
- Goals: Business expansion, additional income

#### Secondary Segments
**Large Retailers:**
- 500+ established businesses
- Annual revenue: LYD 5M+
- Technology adoption: High
- Needs: Omnichannel presence, advanced analytics

**International Merchants:**
- Non-Libyan businesses targeting Libyan market
- Focus: Cross-border e-commerce
- Requirements: Multi-currency, international shipping

### Customer Segments
**Primary Customers:**
- Urban millennials (25-35 years)
- Middle-income families
- Tech-savvy professionals
- Geographic focus: Tripoli, Benghazi, Misrata

**Secondary Customers:**
- Rural customers with growing internet access
- Elderly customers (with family assistance)
- Business buyers (B2B transactions)

## Business Requirements

### Functional Requirements

#### Core Platform Features
**Merchant Management:**
- Self-service store creation and management
- Product catalog management (unlimited products)
- Order processing and fulfillment
- Customer relationship management
- Analytics and reporting dashboard

**Customer Experience:**
- Intuitive product discovery and search
- Secure checkout process
- Multiple payment options
- Real-time order tracking
- Customer support integration

**Payment Processing:**
- Moamalat gateway integration
- Cash on delivery processing
- Bank transfer facilitation
- Transaction security and fraud prevention
- Automated reconciliation

#### Advanced Features
**Logistics Integration:**
- Multiple delivery partner APIs
- Real-time shipping tracking
- Automated label generation
- Delivery scheduling
- Returns management

**Marketing Tools:**
- Promotional campaign management
- Customer segmentation
- Email marketing automation
- Social media integration
- SEO optimization tools

### Non-Functional Requirements

#### Performance Requirements
- **Response Time**: <2 seconds for page loads
- **Uptime**: 99.9% platform availability
- **Concurrent Users**: Support 10,000+ simultaneous users
- **Transaction Processing**: Handle 1,000+ transactions/minute
- **Mobile Performance**: Optimized for 3G/4G networks

#### Security Requirements
- **Data Encryption**: AES-256-GCM for sensitive data
- **Payment Security**: PCI DSS compliance
- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions
- **Audit Logging**: Comprehensive security logging
- **Regular Security Audits**: Quarterly penetration testing

#### Scalability Requirements
- **User Growth**: Support 1M+ users within 2 years
- **Data Storage**: Petabyte-scale data management
- **API Throughput**: 100,000+ API calls/minute
- **Global Expansion**: Multi-region deployment capability

### Technical Requirements

#### Infrastructure Needs
**Cloud Hosting:**
- Railway for backend deployment
- Vercel for frontend hosting
- MySQL database with high availability
- CDN for global content delivery
- Redis for caching and sessions

**Development Stack:**
- Frontend: React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- Backend: Node.js, Express, Sequelize ORM
- Database: MySQL with connection pooling
- Security: JWT, bcrypt, encryption libraries
- Testing: Jest, automated testing suite

#### Integration Requirements
**Payment Gateways:**
- Moamalat primary integration
- Future: Fawry, PayPal, international gateways
- Webhook support for real-time updates
- Reconciliation and settlement automation

**Logistics Partners:**
- Aramex API integration
- Libyan Post service integration
- Local courier partnerships
- International shipping providers
